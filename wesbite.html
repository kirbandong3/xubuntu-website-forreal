Why do we love dogs so much?

Dogs are beautiful and majestic creatures. They are like us, created by God. In fact, god spelled backwards is dog. We share a common similarity, we are loyal to one another. Dogs show a great sign of loyalty. If they are treated and raised correctly, there will be no doubt that they will become your best friend. They understand how we are feeling. They are emphathetic and can read our emotions. They will cheer us up with their company and encourage us to be active. Some of the company dogs provide us include going on walks and playing catch. Not only do we love dogs, but we also love the fact that they make us responsible to them as they are dependent on us, which makes us feel like we are important to them, like a parent.
